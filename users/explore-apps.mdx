## Finding Apps

### App Store Homepage

The main page shows all launched apps with:
- **App icon and name**
- **Token symbol** (e.g., $NPONG)
- **Status**: Raising or Live
- **Creator address**

### App Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Raising** | Bonding curve active, price discovery phase |
| **Live** | Graduated to DEX, free market trading |

## Playing Apps

Most Elata apps run directly in your browser.

### Getting Started

1. Go to the app's detail page
2. Click "Play" button
3. App loads in embedded view
4. Click full-screen for better experience

### Connecting Your Wallet

Some apps need wallet connection for:
- Saving progress
- Accessing premium features
- Participating in tournaments
- Verifying item ownership

### EEG Integration

Apps may support EEG devices:
- Check app description for compatibility
- Elata EEG device recommended
- Third-party devices may work

## Be Careful

- **Start small** — learn how it works before going big
- **Check the creator** — look up their address, see their history
- **Verify links** — scammers impersonate in Discord/Telegram
- **Accept the risk** — tokens can go to zero, most apps won't moon
